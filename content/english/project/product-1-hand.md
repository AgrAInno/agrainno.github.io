## Inno Handheld: Smart Sensor for Plant Nitrogen Monitoring

The Inno Handheld is a portable device designed for rapid, non-destructive assessment of leaf nitrogen concentration in crops. Developed by AgrAInno, this device combines advanced spectral sensing with a user-friendly design, enabling farmers and researchers to collect accurate nitrogen data in the field within seconds.

The device uses proprietary algorithms to interpret reflectance data and deliver actionable insights for optimized nutrient management. Whether used independently or in combination with drone and satellite platforms, the Inno Handheld plays a key role in precision agriculture.
