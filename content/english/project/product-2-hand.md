## Inno Handheld 2.0: Bluetooth-Enabled Smart Sensor for Plant Nutrition

The Inno Handheld 2.0 is the next-generation portable sensor developed by AgrAInno for accurate, non-destructive nitrogen concentration measurement in crops. Equipped with Bluetooth connectivity, this device seamlessly pairs with mobile apps to provide real-time nitrogen data in the field.

Designed for agronomists, researchers, and precision farmers, the sensor combines advanced optical measurement technology with a compact ergonomic form. It supports seamless data synchronization with AgrAInno’s cloud platform, enabling users to track crop status and optimize fertilizer use with ease.

### Key Features:
- Bluetooth pairing with Android/iOS apps for data access and visualization
- High-precision spectral sensing technology
- Durable field-ready design with intuitive controls
- Integration with AgrAInno’s nutrient decision support system

### Applications:
- Field-based nitrogen assessment in maize, wheat, and vegetable crops
- Fertilizer planning and application timing
- Agronomic trials and sustainable agriculture research

With Inno Handheld 2.0, AgrAInno is advancing plant nutrition diagnostics to help build a more productive and environmentally responsible farming future.
